Hello - I am Tobi Akinola

And I am an aspiring programmer :)

A little bit about me..

I was born and raised in the Greater East London area, residing in a small part of London
called Ilford for the majority of my life. I went to local schools, eventually completing
my A-levels in sciences and maths to go on and complete an engineering degree at
Lancaster University!

After graduating in 2019, I enrolled into an engineering masters program and shortly entered the corporate world after,
specifically in auditing, in late 2021. After a year and a half in that role, I finally decided to take the plunge
and persue what I felt was more suited to me and my talents and personality - software development,
which has led me here today!

Why programming?

I have found that I like the fact that programming allows me to produce something from nothing and bring ideas and thoughts to life.
To me, it is almost a superpower to be able to build a website, mobile app, game, or any other digital product that one can imagine.
Programming challenges my problem-solving skills, as one needs to break down complex problems into smaller, manageable steps and
find a logical solution - And there is always a solution.

Programming also pushes me to innovate and dream to create new solutions that will have a positive impact on the world.
By learning programming, young individuals can become agents of change and contribute to technological advancements
that will continue to change the world as we know it.
